Whitewater Adventures

.

Founded in 2005, Wild Rapids Adventures began as a small, passionate team of outdoor enthusiasts who shared a common dream: to introduce people to the exhilarating world of whitewater rafting. What started as a humble operation with just a handful of rafts and a commitment to safety and fun has since evolved into a leading adventure company known for its exceptional experiences and dedication to environmental stewardship.

.

History



  • The company was born in the picturesque region of the Rocky Mountains, where the rivers flow with untamed energy and breathtaking scenery. Our founders, who were avid rafters themselves, recognized the need for a guiding service that prioritized both adventure and safety. They set out to create a company that would not only provide thrilling rafting experiences but also educate participants about the importance of preserving the natural beauty of the rivers and surrounding ecosystems.
Over the years, Wild Rapids Adventures has expanded its offerings, introducing a variety of rafting packages that cater to all skill levels, from family-friendly floats to adrenaline-pumping Class V rapids. Our commitment to excellence has earned us numerous awards and accolades, as well as a loyal customer base that returns year after year to experience the thrill of the rapids.
